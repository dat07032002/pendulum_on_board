## 2. KEY FINDINGS

### 2.1 🔴 ROOT CAUSE: `set_attr` never reached `FurutaEnv` (see §0)
- The "DR eval is unreliable" symptom was traced to its root: `VecEnv.set_attr` (used by both the
  Curriculum and the eval setup) writes to the Monitor wrapper, NOT the env. So the eval ran with
  `tilt_amp=0` (no tilt) → reported DR-only swing-up ~0.71 vs the real ±20° tilt ~0.22; and the
  curriculum never changed the task (tilt/init-angle/randomize/arm_center_w all stayed at defaults).
- Diagnostic chain that pinned it: (a) `evaluations.npz` showed SD=0.22 (too high for 100 indep eps)
  + a 0.82 spike → noise+selection; (b) per-seed vs continuous independent eval both ~0.22 (stable, so
  not my eval); (c) replicating the trainer's exact eval reproduced 0.71; (d) inspecting the eval env
  showed `FurutaEnv.tilt_amp==0` after `set_attr` → tilt never applied. **FIX (applied):** `set_params`
  + `env_method`. **Always re-verify with `rl/eval_policy.py` (sets attrs directly on FurutaEnv).**

### 2.2 Peak-then-collapse is NOT entropy
- It recurs with `ent_coef` FIXED at 0.05 (auto-tuner disabled, verified). So it's not entropy.
- Most likely **TQC critic value overestimation**. Next lever: `top_quantiles_to_drop_per_net` 2→3
  (more conservative critic). Keep-best + `--stop_success` *mitigate* (capture peak) but don't *cure*.

### 2.3 Real difficulty
- Verified tilt performance is low (±30°: ~0.40 free; ±20°: ~0.27 free) — the corner-weighted DR
  (`p_corner=0.3`) + tilt is near the ±6 V authority limit. Policies handle easy draws, fail corners.
- **cable + tilt ≫ harder than free-arm + tilt** (holding true-vertical under tilt drifts the arm into
  the ±180° cable). Raising `arm_center_w` to 0.15 for the cable helped *training-eval* but real perf
  stayed low. Free-arm is sim-only (real rig has the cable).

### 2.4 What works
- **Nominal swing-up+balance is solved & robust** (0.97/0.87). The two-phase split is the right
  structure: learn the skill cleanly (no DR) → robustify (warm-start). Warm-start is FAST.
- **`--stop_success` + no-centering + n_eval=100** gave a clean nominal master with no wasted compute
  (s0 stopped at 280k @0.96). Multi-seed + keep-best handles the high seed variance.

### 2.5 `--stop_success` gating bug (minor)
- It only fires at the final curriculum stage (`max_stage`). But the eval env is ALWAYS at the target
  tilt regardless of curriculum stage, so a warm-started policy can hit threshold *early* (s4 hit 0.82
  on the eval at step 60k while still ramping) and the stop won't fire. **Fix: drop the stage gate**
  (stop whenever the target-condition eval crosses the threshold). Keep-best covered for it this time.

### 2.6 Geometry (CORRECTED) — for Phase-2 hardware
- φ=0 (cable-center, benign): **arm ⟂ tilt axle**, pole swings ALONG the axle (tilt can't drive it).
- φ=90° (hard): arm ∥ axle, pole swings ⟂ (tilt drives it max).
- **Mount the real rig so the arm-rest/zero points PERPENDICULAR to the LX-16A tilt axle.** (Sim is
  already correct; only my earlier verbal advice was inverted.)
